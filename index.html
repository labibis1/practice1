Celebrity Edge, operated by Celebrity Cruises, will enter Colombo from Kochi today. The ship, with 2,780 passengers and 1,273 crew members, will set sail for Colombo, Sri Lanka, by 5pm. The passengers – mostly hailing from America, Australia and Canada – will embark on a guided sightseeing tour in and around Kochi and Sri Lanka. Celebrity Cruises has engaged around eight operators in the city to facilitate local tours. (Foreign Agencies)
